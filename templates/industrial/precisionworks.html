Who We Are

Precision is not a feature — it's the whole job

Since 2011, Precision Works has machined critical components for the aerospace, medical-device and semiconductor sectors. Our Kaki Bukit facility pairs modern multi-axis CNC equipment with disciplined in-house inspection.

  • Aluminium, stainless steel and titanium specialists
  • First-article inspection on every production batch
  • Rapid prototyping with full-run capacity to match

What We Do

Complete precision machining, in-house

CNC Machining

3- and 4-axis milling for complex geometries and tight tolerances.

5-Axis Milling

Single-setup machining of intricate, multi-face components.

Precision Turning

CNC lathe work for shafts, bushings and rotational parts.

EDM Wire-Cut

Wire-cut electrical discharge machining for fine, hardened profiles.

Surface Grinding

Flat and profile grinding for superior surface finishes.

Inspection & QA

CMM measurement and full dimensional reporting on request.
