Who We Are

Fabricating with precision since 1998

Seng Hin Engineering is a Singapore sheet-metal fabrication specialist. From our Tuas workshop we bring fibre-laser cutting, CNC bending, rolling, shearing and welding together with hands-on engineering support — so your drawings become finished parts quickly and consistently, whether it's a single prototype or a production run.

  • Customised sheet-metal fabrication, prototype to batch
  • Stainless steel, mild steel & aluminium under one roof
  • In-house engineering & DFM support on every job
