Workplace Safety

bizSafe accreditation

bizSafe is Singapore's 5-step programme by the WSH Council that helps companies build workplace safety & health capability. We are accredited to bizSafe Star — the highest tier.

1
Level 1

Top management attends the bizSafe workshop and commits to workplace safety & health.

2
Level 2

A risk-management champion is trained to lead hazard identification and control.

3
Level 3

A documented Risk Management plan is implemented and audited by an approved auditor.

★
bizSafe Star

A full WSH Management System certified to SS ISO 45001 — the highest bizSafe level.
