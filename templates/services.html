About Us

Singapore's Trusted SME Accounting Partner Since 2003

Apex Business Consulting was founded with a clear mission: to give Singapore's small and medium enterprises access to the same quality of financial and compliance expertise enjoyed by large corporations — at fees that make sense for SMEs.

Based at International Plaza in the heart of Singapore's CBD, our bilingual team of Chartered Accountants and ACRA-registered agents handles everything from bookkeeping to GST filing, corporate tax, and company secretarial work.

Our Services

Everything Your Singapore Business Needs

End-to-end financial and corporate services under one roof — so you can focus on running your business.

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Accurate, timely financial records prepared by Chartered Accountants — monthly or quarterly, to SFRS standards.

  • Monthly/quarterly management accounts
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Accounts payable & receivable
  • Financial statement preparation

Corporate Tax

Maximise your tax savings with expert IRAS compliance, optimised deductions, and strategic planning for your corporate tax filings.

  • Form C-S / Form C preparation
  • Tax exemption scheme advisory
  • Capital allowances & deductions
  • IRAS correspondence handling

GST Registration & Filing

From voluntary registration to quarterly F5/F7 returns, we keep your GST obligations fully compliant with IRAS requirements.

  • GST registration & deregistration
  • Quarterly F5 / F7 return filing
  • Input tax claim review
  • IRAS GST audit support

Corporate Secretarial

ACRA-registered company secretaries handling your statutory obligations accurately and on time, every time.

  • Company incorporation (Pte Ltd)
  • Annual return filing (ACRA)
  • Director & shareholder changes
  • Registered office address

Payroll Services

Accurate monthly payroll processing including CPF contributions, IR8A preparation, and MOM compliance for all employee types.

  • Monthly payslip generation
  • CPF submission (employees & PR)
  • IR8A & IR21 filing
  • Leave & claims management

Business Advisory

Strategic financial guidance to help your SME grow sustainably — from cash flow analysis to grant application support and business restructuring.

  • Cash flow forecasting
  • Enterprise Development Grant (EDG)
  • Business restructuring advice
  • Financial due diligence
