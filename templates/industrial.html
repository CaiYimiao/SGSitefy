Who We Are

A precision fabrication partner you can rely on

Seng Hin Engineering has fabricated precision metal components in Singapore since 1998. From our Tuas workshop we combine fibre-laser cutting, CNC bending, rolling and welding with hands-on engineering — turning your drawings into finished parts with speed and consistency.

  • Customised sheet-metal fabrication, prototype to batch
  • Stainless steel, mild steel & aluminium under one roof
  • In-house engineering & DFM support on every job

What We Do

Complete fabrication, in-house

Every process under one roof — so your parts are cut, formed, joined and finished without ever leaving our workshop.

Processing

Laser Cutting

High-precision fibre-laser profiles in steel, stainless and aluminium.

Metal Bending

Metal Rolling

Metal Shearing

Fast, accurate shearing to size raw sheet and plate.
