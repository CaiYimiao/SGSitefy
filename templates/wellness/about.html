Who We Are

Born from a belief in mindful beauty

Serene Aesthetics & Wellness was founded in 2009 by Dr Lena Tan, a CIDESCO-certified therapist who saw a gap in Singapore's beauty landscape — a place that cared equally for skin science and the soul. Today our team of eight specialist therapists serves thousands of clients a year from our purpose-designed studio in Ngee Ann City, Orchard Road.

We believe radiant skin and genuine wellbeing are inseparable. Every treatment we offer is designed with both in mind — using only clean, clinically tested products chosen for Singapore's tropical climate.

Our Philosophy

Three pillars of serene beauty

Every decision we make — from the treatments we design to the products we stock — is guided by these core beliefs.

✦

Science-led care

We invest in the latest evidence-based technologies — from medical-grade LED to advanced enzyme exfoliation — so every treatment delivers real, measurable results, not just relaxation.

◎

Holistic harmony

Skin health is whole-person health. We weave stress-relief and mindfulness into every session, because a calm mind is the foundation of glowing skin.

❤

Clean beauty always

Every product in our studio is cruelty-free, free of parabens and unnecessary harsh chemicals, and selected for its efficacy in our humid tropical environment.
