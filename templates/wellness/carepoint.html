About the Clinic

A clinic that feels like family

Since 2012, CarePoint Family Clinic has been a familiar face in the Ang Mo Kio heartland. Our doctors take the time to listen, explain clearly and treat you the way they'd treat their own family — practical, affordable, no rushing. Three generations of neighbours have trusted us with their everyday health.

Our Services

Everyday care, all under one roof

From the common cold to long-term condition management, our family doctors handle the health needs of your whole household.

🩺

General Consultation

Coughs, colds, fevers, infections and everyday ailments — seen promptly by a caring GP.

📋

Health Screening

Comprehensive screening packages including blood tests, ECG and lifestyle assessments.

💉

Vaccinations

Childhood, travel and seasonal flu vaccinations for the whole family, all on schedule.

❤

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care for diabetes, hypertension and cholesterol under the CHAS Chronic scheme.

✚

Minor Procedures

Wound dressing, suturing, removal of cysts and other minor surgical procedures in-clinic.

🏠

House Calls

Home visits for elderly and home-bound patients within the Ang Mo Kio area.
