Our Story

Four Decades of Hardware Expertise

Founded in 1985 by Mr Tan Ah Kow, Lian Huat Hardware began as a small stall in Toa Payoh selling basic hand tools to local contractors. Today, the second generation runs a full-service hardware and industrial supplies depot — without ever losing the personal touch that made us famous.

We stock over 5,000 SKUs across hand tools, power tools, fasteners, safety gear, electrical fittings, plumbing, and industrial consumables — all at transparent trade pricing with no minimum order for walk-in customers.
