Getting Here

We're tucked into the ground floor of a pre-war shophouse on Eng Hoon Street — look for the red awning and the smell of fresh bread.

By MRT
Tiong Bahru MRT (EW17) — 8-minute walk via Tiong Bahru Road.

By Bus
Routes 16, 33, 63, 122, 175 — stop at Tiong Bahru Plaza.

Parking
Public carpark at Tiong Bahru Plaza (5 min walk). Street parking along Eng Hoon Street on weekday mornings.

See you at the Bakehouse

Come in for breakfast, stay for a second coffee — the sourdough and the seats are always waiting.
