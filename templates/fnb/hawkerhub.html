Our Story

A family stall with a big appetite for tradition

Hawker Hub began in 2018 when the Tan family brought Grandpa's char kway teow recipe to a humble unit at Old Airport Road. Today we still fire every order in cast-iron woks over roaring flames — the only way real hawker food should taste.

  • Family recipes passed down three generations
  • Fresh ingredients sourced daily from the wet market
  • Halal certified — everyone's welcome at our table
