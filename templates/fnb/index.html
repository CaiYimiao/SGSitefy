Our Story

A neighbourhood bakery with a big heart

Born from a shared passion for honest bread and good coffee, Tiong Bahru Bakehouse has been a cherished corner of Singapore's oldest housing estate since 2014. Every loaf is long-fermented, every pastry hand-laminated, every cup brewed with care.

  • Stone-milled heritage flours, sourced responsibly
  • Open kitchen — watch the bakers at work every morning
  • Bilingual menu & team — welcoming all neighbours
