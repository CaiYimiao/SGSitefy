Our Story

Serving Singapore's trade since 1985

Lian Huat Hardware was founded in 1985 by Mr Tan Ah Kow, a former toolmaker with a simple belief: tradespeople deserve a supplier who understands the job. Starting as a small counter in Toa Payoh, we have grown into one of Singapore's most trusted hardware and industrial supplies houses — stocking over 5,000 products from the world's leading brands.

Today, the second generation of the Tan family continues that legacy — bringing the same hands-on knowledge and personal service to contractors, MRO buyers, factories, and individual craftsmen across the island.

  • 40 years in Singapore's hardware trade
  • Authorised stockist for Bosch, Stanley, Makita, DeWalt & more
  • Family-run with second-generation ownership
  • Walk-in and bulk trade customers welcome
  • Knowledgeable staff who know the trade inside-out
