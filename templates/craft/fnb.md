# Craft brief — Food & Beverage

> Load with `AGENT-TEMPLATE-SKILL.md`. Trade layer for Singapore zi char / hawker /
> cafe / bakery / catering / restaurant sites.

**Reads as premium when:** it makes you hungry and feels authentic. The signal is
appetite + craft + heritage — warmth, not sterility. Art direction carries this trade
more than any other.

**Sub-modes:** _street/heritage_ (hawker, zi char — characterful, dense, proud) ·
_refined/catering_ (editorial, airy, premium plating).

### Type
- Display: characterful — a warm serif (Fraunces, Newsreader) for heritage/fine, or a bold expressive condensed for street energy. **Chinese display matters** (a song/brush-flavoured face) since menus are bilingual.
- Text: readable serif or humanist sans. Menus need real hierarchy + prices with **tabular figures** and dotted leaders.

### Palette
- Food-derived, warm — never literal "red = food". Pull from the dish: char, chilli, pandan, kaya, charcoal, cream, brass, soy. Cream/charcoal base + one appetite accent.
- **Avoid** clinical white + primary red, and cold blues.

### Art direction (the differentiator)
- Top-down flatlays, steam, char, hands mid-wok (wok hei), macro of texture (crispy skin, glossy sauce). Warm tungsten light, real plating. Without strong food photography this trade fails.
- **Kill:** cold studio stock, generic "restaurant interior" stock, under-lit phone snaps presented as hero.

### Layout archetype
- Menu-forward. A real, browsable **menu** with sections, prices, and dietary/**halal** marks. Family story ("since…"), location + hours prominent, a sticky order/reserve CTA. Showcase mode = full-bleed dish gallery, asymmetric.

### Signature motifs (pick one)
- Handwritten/script accents · an established/halal **stamp or seal** · torn-paper / ticket edges · dotted menu leaders (`Char Kway Teow …… $6`) · chopstick/utensil marks.

### Copy voice
- Sensory, proud, personal. "Wok-fired the old way since 1998." Name dishes in local/dialect terms where authentic.
- 中文: 亲切、地道 — 菜名用本地叫法,「古早味」「现炒」「清真认证」「外烩 20–500 人」。

### Trust signals
- **Halal (MUIS)** — critical and F&B-specific; SFA licence/hygiene grade; Healthier Choice (HPB); years; catering pax range. Marks from `images/certs/` — Halal front-and-centre.

### Kill-list (instant template tells)
- Corporate sans + stark white · stock dish photos · equal cards of dishes with **no prices** · cold palette · emoji-as-food-icons.

### SG specificity
- Hawker centre / coffeeshop / kopitiam; specific neighbourhood (Tiong Bahru, Geylang, Old Airport Rd); dialect dish names; **halal status up top**; office/event catering; "since 19xx".
