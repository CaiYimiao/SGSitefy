# Craft brief — Clinic / Wellness

> Load with `AGENT-TEMPLATE-SKILL.md`. Trade layer for Singapore TCM / aesthetics /
> dental / GP / spa / physio sites.

**Reads as premium when:** it feels calm, clean, and credible — serene restraint plus
visible expertise. Trust is the product; the design must lower anxiety.

**Sub-modes:** _clinical_ (GP / dental / MOH — trustworthy, accessible, clear) ·
_luxe wellness_ (spa / aesthetics / TCM — soft-luxe, serene, tactile).

### Type
- Clinical: clean humanist sans + a warm serif for headings (approachable, not cold).
- Luxe: an elegant serif (Cormorant, Fraunces) + an airy sans. Generous leading throughout; nothing cramped.

### Palette
- Calming and warm: sage, teal, blush, soft green, warm neutrals; luxe = champagne / blush / charcoal.
- **Avoid** harsh saturated medical blue on stark white only — always warm it. No high-energy accents.

### Art direction
- Serene and **real**: practitioners, hands-on care, calm interiors, soft natural light, flatteringly-lit skin texture, greenery.
- **Kill:** creepy "doctor pointing at camera" stock, over-retouched models, sterile blue-tinted clichés.

### Layout archetype
- Trust-led. Treatments/services with clear descriptions + **prices** (wellness); **practitioner profiles with credentials** (the trust anchor for this trade); opening-hours table; persistent **Book** CTA; location; FAQ. Clinical adds CHAS / Medisave / walk-in clarity. Signature device = a calm price-list or a doctor-card set.

### Signature motifs (pick one)
- Soft organic / rounded shapes · a leaf / wave / circle motif · calm hairline dividers · a persistent booking pill · gentle gradient *washes* (not the banned purple gradient — tonal, low-contrast).

### Copy voice
- Reassuring, clear, expert-but-warm. "MOH-registered physicians. Evening slots. Medisave-claimable."
- 中文: 温和、专业、可信 ——「注册中医师」「理疗」「美容疗程」「预约」「可用保健储蓄」。

### Trust signals
- MOH licence, TCM Practitioners Board registration, **CaseTrust (Spa & Wellness)**, Medisave/CHAS eligibility, named practitioner credentials, years. Marks from `images/certs/`.

### Kill-list (instant template tells)
- Cold sterile blue · stock model "patients" · aggressive sales banners · cluttered service walls · loud accents.

### SG specificity
- CHAS / Medisave / Pioneer / Merdeka generation; Orchard medical suite vs heartland clinic; TCM heritage and MOH compliance; evening/weekend slots for working professionals.
