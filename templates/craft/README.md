template, load the matching brief** — it takes the general "hand-made signal" rules
down to what reads as bespoke for that specific Singapore trade (type, palette, art
direction, layout archetype, signature motifs, copy voice EN+中文, trust signals,
the kill-list of template tells, and local SG context).
