# Craft brief — Professional Services

> Load with `AGENT-TEMPLATE-SKILL.md`. Trade layer for Singapore law / accounting /
> corporate-secretarial / consultancy / agency sites.

**Reads as premium when:** it carries authority, trust, and clarity. The signal is
gravitas through restraint and credentials — fewer words, larger, with proof.

**Sub-modes:** _prestige_ (law / accounting / finance — authoritative, heritage, serif) ·
_modern_ (agency / tech consult — bold, confident, sans, outcome-led).

### Type
- Prestige: a refined serif (Playfair Display, Spectral, Fraunces) for authority + a quiet sans for body; credentials in **tabular figures**.
- Modern: a strong contemporary grotesque, set big and confident, with lots of white.

### Palette
- Prestige: deep and sober — emerald / navy / oxblood + a **gold or brass** accent, off-white parchment ground.
- Modern: near-black + one bold accent, abundant white.
- **Avoid** the generic corporate-blue gradient and stock "trust blue".

### Art direction
- Restrained and real: considered **team portraits** (real people = the trust signal in services), the office / boardroom, the CBD, or tasteful abstract; modern mode can use type-as-image and case-study visuals.
- **Kill:** handshake / skyscraper / "global network" stock, fake diverse-team stock.

### Layout archetype
- Credibility-led. Practice areas / services as a **numbered index**; **partner/team profiles with credentials**; recognition / awards; case studies with **metrics** (modern); process steps; a consultation CTA. Signature device = a numbered practice-area index, a partner-card set, or an oversized metrics band.

### Signature motifs (pick one)
- Prestige: hairline rules · a serif drop-cap · numbered sections · a monogram / seal · a single gold accent line.
- Modern: oversized stats · a process arrow · a bold section index.

### Copy voice
- Prestige: measured, authoritative, precise — "Counsel for complex cross-border matters since 1998." Modern: sharp, outcome-led — "We grew their qualified pipeline 3× in two quarters."
- 中文: 正式、专业 ——「公司秘书」「税务咨询」「跨境事务」「企业顾问」; prestige register more formal.

### Trust signals
- Years, number of partners / professionals, ISO 9001 / **27001** (services), **ISCA** (accounting), Law Society / professional-body membership, awards, named sectors, ACRA-registered. Marks from `images/certs/`.

### Kill-list (instant template tells)
- Stocky corporate imagery · "solutions / synergy / empower" filler · blue gradient · equal service cards with vague copy · no real names or credentials.

### SG specificity
- Raffles Place / Shenton Way / Marina CBD address; ACRA / IRAS / MAS context; cross-border / regional (SEA) positioning; professional-body memberships; "since 19xx" or "Big-4 trained".
