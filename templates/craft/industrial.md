# Craft brief — Industrial / Engineering

> Load with `AGENT-TEMPLATE-SKILL.md`. This is the trade-specific layer: what makes
> a Singapore fabrication / precision / marine / O&G / MRO site read as *earned
> competence* rather than a generic business template.

**Reads as premium when:** it shows rigour and scale, not gloss. The signal is "we
make the part that holds the bridge up" — precision, proof, no fluff. Restraint +
real process imagery beats any gradient.

**Sub-modes:** _heavy-industrial_ (raw, dark, workshop) · _precision/clean_ (white,
metrology, semicon/aerospace tolerances).

### Type
- Display: structural — a condensed or industrial grotesque (Archivo, Saira Condensed, Anton for heavy) or a slab; technical accents in a **mono** (IBM Plex Mono, Space Mono).
- Text: neutral humanist grotesque with **tabular figures** — specs, tolerances and dimensions must align in columns.
- Treatment: set big headings tight; technical labels UPPERCASE + letterspaced; use mono for part numbers, tolerances, capacities.

### Palette
- Pull from materials: steel greys, raw concrete, gunmetal; one restrained safety accent (amber/hazard-orange, weld-blue, oxide-red). Dark workshop themes are legitimate.
- Off-black ink (`#14110f`–`#1b1d22`), off-white paper. **Avoid corporate baby-blue, friendly purples, and any gradient.**

### Art direction
- Real process at scale: sparks, machined surfaces, a weld bead macro, CAD→finished part, the shop floor wide. Hard directional light, slight grain, cool grade.
- **Kill stock:** no smiling handshakes, no glossy generic 3D renders (only their own CAD), no laptop-and-coffee.

### Layout archetype
- Spec-sheet logic. A **capabilities matrix** (materials × processes × tolerances) is the hero device. Numbered process steps (cut → form → weld → finish → QC). A hard stat band (since-year · tonnes/yr · parts/yr · ISO). Blueprint/grid as texture.

### Signature motifs (pick one, thread it)
- Dimension/tolerance annotations as graphics (`⌀`, `±0.1 mm`, datum flags) · engineering crosshairs/registration marks · oversized process numerals · faint blueprint grid.

### Copy voice
- Precise, declarative, metric. "Fibre-laser cut to ±0.1 mm. 5-axis. Any order size, since 1998." No adjectives where a number works.
- 中文: 务实、精准的工程用语 —「按图定制」「公差 ±0.1mm」「一站式加工」「不设最低订量」。

### Trust signals
- ISO 9001 / 14001 / 45001, bizSAFE (Star/L3), SAC accreditation; sectors served (marine, O&G, semicon, aerospace); no-minimum-order; lead times; established year. Use the cert marks from `images/certs/`.

### Kill-list (instant template tells)
- Gradient hero · rounded friendly cards · emoji icons · "innovative solutions / world-class" · generic blue · lifestyle stock · 4 equal feature cards.

### SG specificity
- Tuas / Jurong / Sungei Kadut / Gul addresses; "since 19xx"; family-run + precision pride; SAC-SINGLAS, WSH, named local sectors. This is what tells a Singapore buyer "they're real."
