# Craft brief — Retail / Trading

> Load with `AGENT-TEMPLATE-SKILL.md`. Trade layer for Singapore retail boutiques and
> B2B trading / distribution. **This trade has two genuinely different modes — pick one
> from the description and commit; do not blend them.**

**Reads as premium when:** _consumer retail_ feels like an aspirational lookbook you
can shop; _B2B trading_ feels like a credible, deep-stock supplier you can rely on.

### Mode A — Consumer retail / boutique
- **Type:** fashion-editorial — high-contrast serif (Cormorant, Fraunces) + a clean quiet sans, lots of air.
- **Palette:** brand-led, often near-monochrome + one pop colour.
- **Art direction:** lifestyle + crisp product-on-seamless, one consistent set/grade; a **lookbook** gallery is the centrepiece.
- **Layout:** featured products **with prices**, category pills, "new arrivals" strip, lookbook, store + hours. Editorial spacing around the product grid (not a cramped dropship grid).
- **Motifs:** price tags, "new" ribbon, editorial item numbering.
- **Trust:** **CaseTrust** (consumer trust), returns/exchange policy, payment marks, islandwide delivery.

### Mode B — B2B trading / distributor
- **Type:** utilitarian clarity — a strong grotesque, **tabular figures** for SKUs/prices/MOQ.
- **Palette:** restrained corporate-but-warm; a trustworthy accent (not baby-blue); colour-code categories.
- **Art direction:** clean product / warehouse / category imagery; iconography for categories ok if custom.
- **Layout:** **category index with item counts**, bulk/RFQ CTA, trusted-by logos, stock-breadth stats (SKUs · brands · years · clients), request-a-quote form.
- **Motifs:** SKU codes, numbered category index, an RFQ/quote device.
- **Trust:** years in trade, SKU count, brands carried, trade account / net-30, bizSAFE, ISO 9001.

### Copy voice
- Retail: aspirational, tight, brand-voiced. B2B: plain and specific — "5,000 SKUs · same-day before 2pm · net-30 for trade".
- 中文: 零售走品牌调性；批发务实 ——「现货充足」「批量优惠」「全岛配送」「企业账户」。

### Kill-list (instant template tells)
- Generic dropship-template grid · product cards with **no price** · stock smiling-shopper photos · rainbow palette · blending the two modes into a vague "shop".

### SG specificity
- Retail: ION / Orchard / Bugis vs heartland; GST-registered; PayNow/cards. B2B: Soon Lee / Toa Payoh / Kaki Bukit industrial; `.sg` trust; islandwide; GST + UEN shown.
