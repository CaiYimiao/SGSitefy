Our Story 我们的故事

Rooted in Tradition, Risen with Craft 传承根基，匠心成就

Born in 2017 in a century-old shophouse on Eng Hoon Street, Tiong Bahru Bakehouse was founded by baker-couple Mei Lin and James Tan — inspired by their grandparents' kopitiam heritage and European baking apprenticeships. 2017年，烘焙夫妇林美玲与陈杰明在恩厚街一座百年老店屋里创立了中峇鲁烘焙坊，他们的灵感来自祖辈的咖啡馆传统与欧洲烘焙学徒经历。

Every loaf is cold-retarded for a minimum of 16 hours and baked in a stone deck oven. Our kaya is hand-stirred with fresh pandan from Lim Chu Kang. We source single-origin beans from Thailand's Doi Chang and Java's Ijen Plateau. 每条面包经过至少16小时冷藏发酵，在石板烤箱中烘焙。我们的咖椰酱用来自林厝港的新鲜香兰叶手工搅拌而成，咖啡豆精选自泰国的多依张与爪哇伊真高原。
