Service Line 01

Accounting & Bookkeeping

Accurate, timely financial records are the foundation of every successful business. Our accounting team manages your books to SFRS standards, giving you clean data for decisions and stress-free year-end filing.

Monthly Bookkeeping

Complete transaction recording, bank reconciliation and ledger maintenance.

  • General ledger and chart of accounts setup
  • Monthly bank & credit card reconciliations
  • Accounts payable & receivable management
  • Management accounts with commentary
  • Xero / QuickBooks / MYOB support

Financial Statements

SFRS-compliant annual accounts ready for ACRA filing and audit.

  • Income statement & balance sheet preparation
  • Cash flow statement
  • Notes to financial statements
  • Compilation report for exempt private companies
  • Liaison with appointed auditors

Service Line 02

Tax Advisory & Filing

Singapore's tax landscape offers real opportunities for SMEs — but only if you navigate it correctly. Our IRAS-appointed tax agents minimise your liability within the law and keep you fully compliant.

Corporate Income Tax

Full ECI and Form C/C-S/C-S Lite preparation and e-filing with IRAS.

  • Estimated Chargeable Income (ECI) filing
  • Form C / C-S / C-S Lite preparation
  • Capital allowance & startup exemption claims
  • IRAS query & audit defence support
  • Tax planning & restructuring advice

GST Registration & Returns

Voluntary or compulsory GST registration and quarterly F5/F7 returns.

  • GST registration (voluntary & compulsory)
  • Quarterly GST F5 & F7 return preparation
  • Input tax claim reviews
  • GST health-check and error correction
  • Personal income tax (Form B / B1)

Service Line 03

Corporate Secretarial

Every Singapore company is required by law to maintain a qualified company secretary. Our ICSA-qualified secretaries ensure your statutory obligations are met on time, every time.

Ongoing Secretarial Retainer

Named company secretary service covering all routine annual obligations.

  • Named ICSA-qualified company secretary
  • Annual return filing with ACRA
  • AGM / EGM notices & minutes
  • Statutory registers maintenance
  • Registered office address (optional)

Corporate Changes & Transactions

Director and shareholder changes, share transfers, allotments and capital changes.

  • Director / shareholder appointments & cessations
  • Share transfer instruments & ACRA lodgment
  • Share allotment and rights issue
  • Memorandum & Articles amendments
  • Company name change & striking off

Service Line 04

Payroll & HR Administration

Payroll errors cost time, money and employee trust. Our managed payroll service handles calculations, CPF submissions and IR8A filings so you stay compliant with MOM and IRAS regulations.

Monthly Payroll Processing

Accurate payslips, CPF contributions and leave tracking for your entire headcount.

  • Monthly salary computation & payslip generation
  • CPF submission via CPF EZPay
  • AWS, bonus & commission calculations
  • Annual IR8A / IR8S preparation & AIS submission
  • Foreign worker levy reconciliation

Business Advisory

Strategic guidance on business structure, grant applications and financial planning.

  • Company incorporation advisory (sole prop / Pte Ltd / LLP)
  • EntrePass, EP & S Pass applications
  • Enterprise Development Grant (EDG) support
  • Cash flow forecasting & budgeting
  • Financial due diligence for M&A
