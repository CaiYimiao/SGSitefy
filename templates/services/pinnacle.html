The Firm

A Singapore firm built on judgement, discretion and results

“We measure our work not by the cases we take, but by the outcomes our clients carry forward.”

Founded in 2013 at One Raffles Place, Pinnacle Law LLP has grown into a full-service practice trusted by founders, family offices and listed companies alike. Our lawyers act with the candour of trusted advisers and the resolve of trial advocates.

  • Registered with the Legal Services Regulatory Authority of Singapore

  • Fixed-scope fee arrangements available for most matters
