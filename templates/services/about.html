Our Story

Built for Singapore businesses, from day one

Apex Business Consulting was founded in 2003 by a team of chartered accountants and corporate lawyers who believed that quality compliance services should be accessible to every SME — not just large corporations. Over two decades, we have grown to serve more than 500 businesses across retail, F&B, technology, logistics and professional services, remaining ACRA-registered and independently owned throughout.

  • ACRA-registered public accounting firm since 2003
  • Members of the Institute of Singapore Chartered Accountants (ISCA)
  • MOM-accredited for Employment Pass and work pass advisory
  • Bilingual English and Mandarin service throughout
