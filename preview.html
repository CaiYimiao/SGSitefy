FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before you start.

Do I need any technical skills?

None at all. If you can fill in a form and upload a photo, you can build a site with SGSitefy. The wizard walks you through every step and SitefyAI writes the copy for you.

What if I already have a website?

No problem — paste your existing URL in the "Import from a link" field. SGSitefy pulls your name, contact details, and photos automatically, so you're not starting from scratch.

Can I edit the site after it's published?

Yes. Every field — copy, photos, colours, certifications — is editable from your SGSitefy dashboard. Changes go live in seconds, no re-publishing needed.

Does it work for .sg and .com.sg domains?

Yes. SGSitefy integrates with Cloudflare, Namecheap, and GoDaddy so you can register a .com.sg, .sg, or .com domain right inside the wizard and have it connected automatically.

Is the site bilingual from day one?

Yes — every page is generated in English and Mandarin Chinese simultaneously. SitefyAI writes both versions at once, tuned to your industry's tone in each language.

How does SGSitefy verify my certifications?

It doesn't — and that's intentional. You declare your certifications (BizSAFE, ISO, Halal, etc.) and they appear on your site. You are responsible for the accuracy of what you display. See our Terms of Service for details.

Is my business data safe?

Your UEN and contact details are used only to build your site. We don't sell your data. SGSitefy is PDPA-compliant — see our Privacy Policy for the full picture.

What industries are supported?

Industrial & Engineering, Food & Beverage, Retail / Trading, Clinic / Wellness, and Professional Services. Each has a hand-crafted template — more industries are added regularly.
